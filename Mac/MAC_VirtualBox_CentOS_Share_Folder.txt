========================
CentOS share folder setting
========================
On Windows, you simply click "Devices -> Insert Guest Additions CD images" 

On Linux you need to do the following

가상이미지 설정 - 저장소 - 라이브 cd/dvd - 디스크 아이콘 클릭해서 VBoxGuestAdditions.iso 파일 위치 세팅

/Applications/VirtualBox.app/Contents/MacOS/VBoxGuestAdditions.iso

yum install gcc* kernel* dkms* make* bzip2 perl*
mkdir /media/VirtualBoxGuestAdditions
mount -r /dev/cdrom /media/VirtualBoxGuestAdditions 
cd /media/VirtualBoxGuestAdditions/
./VBoxLinuxAdditions.run 
reboot
mount -t vboxsf share /root/vbox_share
